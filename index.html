Agent Guide

How Pons IQ Agent works

Pons IQ Agent watches BTC live, locks a timeframe-specific PTB at the start of each market window, then combines price action, volatility, trend and order-flow signals into a final call.

Timeframes 5m / 15m / 1h
PTB Logic Window Start Price
Final Vote Locks Before Expiry
Decision flow
01 The agent reads live BTC price, exchange flow, and indicator data for the active timeframe.
02 It compares BTC to the locked PTB for that specific market window, not a shared price across all tabs.
03 Confidence, volatility, and directional bias update during the window until the final vote locks.
04 After settlement, the result feeds the history, streak, win rate, and performance sections.
Price / PTB Shows the live BTC price, the Polymarket target boundary price, and how far BTC is trading above or below that level. The spread and percent fields tell you how much room remains versus the current market boundary.
Prediction / Volatility Tracks the current directional bias, confidence, countdown to lock, and market volatility before the final vote is fixed. Once the timer reaches the lock point, the final prediction replaces the live bias and should stop changing for that window.
Track Record / Performance Summarizes win rate, streaks, model stats, and recent outcomes so you can judge consistency instead of a single call. Use this area to see whether the model is stable across many windows, not just one good or bad result.
Chart / Technical Snapshot Explains the live BTC chart, RSI, MACD, EMA, VWAP and volume context that support the active timeframe call. This is the section to check whether the prediction is aligned with trend, ranging behavior, and momentum structure.
